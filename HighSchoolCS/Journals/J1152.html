1.1: Why is the test executed prior to the loop?
The test is executed prior to the loop in order to determine which action will happen. If the test evaluates to true, the jump will happen, but if the test evaluates to false, the program executes "alternative."
1.2: Compare this diagram to that of the conditional. What are the similarities? What are the differences?
The flowchart represents the while loop in a more 2 dimensional depiction of the while loop, and the Assembly Language diagram is a more 1 dimensional diagram that has a linear progression and demonstrates how the loop is made.
1.3: What is the purpose of the "Jump" instruction after the "consequent"?
The "Jump" instruction after the "consequent" is used to loop back to "test" and to continue the loop.
