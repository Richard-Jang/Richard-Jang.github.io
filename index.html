Why is the Digital Portfolio Important?

The digital portfolio describes someone, and it shows what they have done. It can track past projects, future dreams or ambitions, and more. It is also convenient for employers to look at the digital portfolio along with a resume. Developing a digital portfolio is also good for website design practice.
