Who Am I?
As a human, I am committed to creating a positive impact. Mentoring is one of the many ways that I try to give back to the community. Some people may not have the luxury of learning opportunities, so I strive to provide a healthy learning environment. My commitments also include being a life-long learner.
As a student at Allen High School, I have been studying fields that I believe will benefit my future as well as for enjoyment. Math and science courses like computer science and statistics have always piqued my interest, but I also love studying fine arts. Music is something I am particularly fond of as I have been in a choral program since the 6th grade. Academics aren't the only thing that I have learned from school though.
As a leader, my peers and teammates are always on my mind. Whether its how I can work better with the people around me, how I can help coordinate my team, or how my teammates and I can individually improve, I try to help in any way possible. Though leadership is an important trait, I have learned that leaders should, first and foremost, be a friend.
As a friend, I care about those around me. Being kind is one of my values. As the social creatures we are, everyone needs a friend. To me, a friend is trustworthy, kind, and reliable, and I aim to be someone who I would consider a friend.

Computer Science Classes

Computer Science I was a class taught by Mrs. Jang, and it my first official class in the Computer Science field. I took the class during my freshmen year of high school (2022-2023), and I learned many skills on top of just computer science. The class increased my critical thinking skills by introducing new technology (such as CoderMerlin's assignment system, Merlin Missions, and working with a Linux terminal) as well as soft skills like working with peers, talking to people, and helping without cheating. The class was mainly target around swift and a small amount of computer architecture, both topics that I wasn't familiar with, but throughout the class, I built up my knowledge on these topics and more.
